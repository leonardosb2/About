Formed in 2013, AHV Communities is the premier developer and builder of single family rental communities. We are fully integrated with a first-class team of experienced professionals. We are relentlessly focused on bottom up cost controls and deal execution, while still maintaining our mission of being the premier provider in this innovative asset class. The Principals of AHV have worked together for over a decade and believe in the value of long term partnerships, fiduciary responsibilities and managing risk while maximizing returns. To date, AHV’s experience includes 15 communities totaling over 2,500 homes with an estimated total capitalization of over $1 billion.
